Six Functions · Research Phase
📈
Sales
Pipeline · Proposals · Conversion
How deals move — or stall
▾
Sales — Research
Process
Map current sales workflow end-to-end
Identify where leads come from and how they convert
Document proposal and follow-up process
Find gaps — where deals are lost and why
Software
Audit CRM or current tracking system
Identify if tools match the actual sales motion
Note any manual workarounds happening
People
Who owns sales — owner or team member?
Is there a clear handoff between sales and delivery?
Who tracks pipeline and holds accountability?
Financials
Review revenue by source or product line
Identify top clients and average deal size
Flag any pricing inconsistencies
Goals
What is the revenue goal for next 90 days / 1 year?
Is there a defined sales target per rep or owner?
What does a "good month" look like?
Project Mgmt
Create sales tracking section in Asana
Document current pipeline stages
Set up recurring sales review cadence
📣
Marketing
Brand · Content · Lead Gen
Who is being reached and how
▾
Marketing — Research
Process
Audit current marketing activities and channels
Map how leads currently find the business
Document content creation and publishing flow
Identify what's working vs. done out of habit
Software
List all marketing tools (email, social, CRM)
Assess if tools are connected or siloed
Review analytics access and what's being tracked
People
Who owns marketing — owner, employee, or agency?
Is there a clear content calendar owner?
Where is the bottleneck — ideas, execution, or time?
Financials
Review current marketing spend
Identify cost per lead if trackable
Flag any spend with no measurable output
Goals
What is the goal — awareness, leads, or retention?
Is there a defined target audience?
What does a successful marketing month look like?
Project Mgmt
Build marketing section in Asana
Document all active campaigns and assets
Set up content calendar framework

Operations

How work moves and where it breaks
▾
Operations — Research
Process
Map every core operational workflow end-to-end
Identify handoffs, dependencies, and bottlenecks
Document where manual workarounds exist
Surface the top 3 recurring operational failures
Software
Audit all ops tools — PM, communication, tracking
Identify what's undocumented or in people's heads

People

Financials

Identify where cost overruns typically happen
Assess if pricing reflects true delivery cost
Goals
What does efficient look like — time, cost, capacity?
What is the biggest operational constraint right now?
What would allow the business to handle 2x volume?
Project Mgmt
Document all active operational workflows in Asana
Map team capacity vs. current workload
Identify top 5 SOPs needed most urgently

Finance

▾
Finance — Research
Process

Financials

Goals

What financial visibility would change decisions most?
Project Mgmt
Document financial review tasks in Asana
Set up recurring bookkeeping task cadence
Log all financial tools and access in Notion

▾

Financials

Goals

Create scorecard metric for sales function
Project Mgmt

Assign task ownership across sales team
