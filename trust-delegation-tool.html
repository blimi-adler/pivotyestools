Step 2
THE TRUST LADDER
Delegation is not one move. It is five levels. Most owners jump from doing everything to full handoff and wonder why it breaks. Tap each level to see what it looks like in practice.

The most common mistake: Jumping from Level 1 to Level 5. You hold everything tightly, hand it off completely, call it empowerment. It breaks. You pull it back. Repeat. The fix is not more trust. It is more structure at each level.
